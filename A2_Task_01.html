Potti Sriramulu Chalavadi Mallikarjuna Rao College of Engineering and Technology (PSCMRCET) is a college located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established in the year 2008 under the aegis of Sri Kanyaka Parameswari Vissamsetti Venkataratnam Hindu High Schools Committee (SKPVVHHSC) by a group of philanthropists belonging to Vijayawada 12. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and management studies.
